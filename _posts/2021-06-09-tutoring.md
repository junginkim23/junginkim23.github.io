#### 오늘의 표현

1. It takes two hours.


#### 오늘의 단어

1. be familiar to 
- meaning : easy to recognize because of being seen, met, heard, etc.
- Example Sentence : This street is familiar to me. 

2. be familiar with
- meaning : to know something or someone well 
- Example Sentence : I am familiar with this street. 

3. Stereotype
- meaning : to characterize or regard as a stereotype
