#### 오늘의 표현

1. They are into k-pop. 
- meaning: They really like k-pop.

2. Do you have long hair? or Are you long-haired? 

3. I'm afriad it won't fit me. 

4. let your hair down.
- meaning : to allow yourself to behave much more freely than usual and enjoy yourself.

5. We don't have enough time. that's really quick.

#### 오늘의 단어

1. hair salon : 미용실

2. three weeks after my haircut. or once in three weeks. or every three weeks.
